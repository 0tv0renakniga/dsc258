# Week 05 
## Overview
* **Summary:**Modern incarnations of language models are not n-gram based- instead they are parameterized by recurrent neural networks (RNNs). This lecture will continue our study of language models and introduce autoregressive neural language models and corresponding learning techniques 
* After this week, you will be able to:
    * Describe the basics of RNN architectures,
    * Describe how an RNN can be used to parameterize a language model
    * Describe the strengths and weaknesses of neural language models vs. their classical n-gram-based counterparts.
